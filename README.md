# Indian Banking Sector Investment Analysis

## Overview
- This project presents an end-to-end analysis of the **Indian Banking Sector** by combining both **Fundamental Analysis** and **Technical Analysis** to evaluate the financial strength, market performance, and investment attractiveness of leading Indian banks.
- The study focuses on comparing **Private Sector Banks** and **Public Sector Banks (PSBs)** to identify sectoral strengths, weaknesses, leaders, laggards, and long-term investment opportunities.
- The analysis aims to support informed decision-making in the context of **Wealth Management and Alternate Investments (WMAI)**.
- This project demonstrates how combining **fundamental and technical analysis** can provide a comprehensive understanding of sector performance and support evidence-based investment decisions. The findings highlight the clear dominance of select private sector banks in terms of wealth creation potential while also identifying tactical opportunities within the public banking space.
